Description
The daily adventure of a coffee bean. Everyday millions of coffee beans are afraid.
This is the exciting and amazing journey of a coffee bean which wants to protect the coffee bean family from the MEAN BEAN MACHINE. One day the big evil giant starts the machine, grabs into the bean bag and throws the coffe bean family member in the machine to grind them! Brave Coffe beans fight against this machine and try to shut down the MEAN BEAN MACHINE. Be a coffee bean and struggle against the morning coffee ritual!
