Description
The daily adventure of a coffeebean. Everyday milliosn of coffeebeans are afraid.
This is the exciting and amazing journey of a coffeebean who want to protect the coffeebean-family ahead the MEAN BEAN MACHINE. One day at a time the big evil giant starts the Machine, grap into the beanbag and throw the coffebeanfamilymember in the machine and grind them! Brave Coffebeans fight against this machine and tries to shut down the BEAN MEAN MACHINE. Be a Coffeebean and struggle against the Morning-Coffee.
